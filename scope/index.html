Scope · boundaries

What we do—and what we do not do.

Clear scope protects participants, volunteers, and partners. We provide support and navigation—not legal or clinical services.

Programs Governance

If you’re in immediate danger or crisis, contact emergency services first.

We are not
  • A law firm or legal counsel
  • A crisis response service
  • A clinic or therapy provider

We do

  • Peer-led cohorts with facilitation and boundaries
  • Rights navigation and documentation readiness
  • Resilience tools and safe referral pathways
  • Pattern-level outreach (de-identified)

We do not

  • Provide legal advice or representation
  • Offer emergency/crisis response
  • Provide clinical therapy or medical care
  • Publicly name individuals/employers through the Society
