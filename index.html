Survivor-led · trauma-informed · BC

A grassroots foundation for dignity, safety, and justice.

We support newcomers and marginalized workers through structured peer cohorts, rights navigation readiness, resilience tools, and de-identified community outreach. Pilot phase now—building capacity for 2026.

View programs Volunteer / collaborate Funding

We do not provide legal representation or clinical therapy. If you are in immediate danger, contact emergency services first.

What we do

  • Facilitated peer cohorts with clear boundaries.
  • Rights navigation & documentation readiness (not legal advice).
  • Resilience tools to reduce isolation and burnout.
  • Safe referral pathways, with consent.

What we don’t do

  • Emergency/crisis response.
  • Clinical therapy or medical services.
  • Legal representation or case management.
