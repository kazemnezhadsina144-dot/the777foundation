The 777 Foundation is building a bridge between lived experience, legal systems, and everyday survival for migrants and vulnerable workers.

Programs

Three core streams work together: emotional safety, justice in action, and storytelling that shifts how institutions listen and respond.

Program · Shielded Souls
Shielded Souls

Trauma-aware support for migrants and workers facing toxic workplaces, wage theft, or systemic neglect – combining peer circles, practical guidance, and curated referrals.

Program · Code 777
Code 777

A living library of rights, real cases, and tools for self-advocacy – turning legal jargon into human language and helping newcomers document, escalate, and protect themselves.

Voice 777

Stories & cases

We publish a carefully anonymised, trauma-aware version of real cases – not to expose people, but to expose patterns. These stories are the engine for change.

This demo page does not list real cases. In the live version, this section will link to:

  • Short narratives about work, housing, and service-provider experiences
  • Plain-language breakdowns of processes (ESB, WSBC, HRT, Ombudsperson, immigration)
  • Resources for community organizations and advocates

For safety, we never share identifying details without explicit consent and layered protection.
