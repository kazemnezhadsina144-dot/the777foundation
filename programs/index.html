Programs · pilot phase

Programs built for safety and practical readiness.

Four streams designed to help reduce isolation, build practical understanding of processes, and support safer engagement with external services.

Ask about programs Scope

No legal representation. No clinical diagnosis or therapy.

Delivery style
  • Small cohorts · structured facilitation · clear scope and safeguarding
  • Privacy-first intake and communication
  • Referral pathways, with consent and privacy safeguards
Stream 1
Peer-led Support Cohorts
Peer connection, shared problem-solving, and practical support—facilitated with clear scope.
Stream 2
Navigation Readiness
Organize timelines, evidence lists, and questions before approaching agencies, service providers, or other formal pathways.
Stream 3
Resilience Tools
Practical coping tools designed to help reduce burnout and isolation during demanding processes.
Stream 4
Community Outreach
De-identified education for allies and communities (privacy- and consent-first).
